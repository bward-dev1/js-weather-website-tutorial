AI Discipline

AI has become something that does not just fix copy/paste errors; as it has become more autonomous, the human role has shifted from a "writer" to a "system architect and ethical gatekeeper." "We must now use AI responsibly and believe that we ourselves can create something it could never do. - We must believe we can make something incredible.

There is a quote by Eliezer Yudkowsky:
"AI doesn't have to be evil to destroy humanity — if AI has a goal and humanity just happens to stand in the way, it will destroy humanity as a matter of course."

— Eliezer Yudkowsky

We must lead the way.
